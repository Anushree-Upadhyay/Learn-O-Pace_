GLAD Test


The GLAD Test (Grade Level Assessment Device) is a specialized tool designed to assess a child's learning abilities and identify areas where additional support may be needed. It helps educators and parents pinpoint specific challenges students face in subjects like reading, writing, and math. With these insights, tailored learning plans can be created to provide the right interventions and strategies for academic success.
